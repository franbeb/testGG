Our Global Projects

Golden Goose Exploration is actively developing promising mineral assets across three continents, focusing on gold, lithium, and rare earth elements.

El Quemado

Salta Province, Argentina

El Quemado is our flagship lithium project located in the heart of South America's "Lithium Triangle." Initial surveys indicate significant lithium-bearing pegmatite formations with favorable extraction characteristics.

Phase: Exploration
Size: 4,200 hectares
Target: Lithium
Est. Completion: 2026
View Details

Rincon Salar

Salta Province, Argentina

The Rincon Salar project is focused on lithium brine extraction using innovative, environmentally-friendly Direct Lithium Extraction (DLE) technologies that minimize water usage and environmental impact in Argentina's renowned "Lithium Triangle".

Phase: Early Exploration
Size: 3,500 hectares
Target: Lithium brine
Est. Resource Def: 2025
View Details

Quebec Claim

James Bay Region, Quebec, Canada

Our Quebec Claim project targets rare earth elements (REE) essential for clean energy technologies and high-tech applications. Initial sampling has revealed significant concentrations of critical rare earths with excellent development potential.

Phase: Early Exploration
Size: 5,800 hectares
Target: Rare Earth Elements
Est. Resource Def: 2026
